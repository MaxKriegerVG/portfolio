Dreamforce (HoloLens)



Dreamforce

(HoloLens)


2017
HoloLens
Unity


An interactive AR trade show demo that I and a team developed for Dreamforce 2017 using a Microsoft HoloLens and Unity. I was responsible for the 3D implementation and UI design/functionality.

In this demo, used to demonstrate a potential AR training scenario powered by Salesforce, users are guided through the task of customizing and assembling a Lego minifigure. Real-time data, like the amount of parts remaining for each option, is pulled down from a Salesforce cloud via REST web service.

I'm a game developer, graphic designer, and multimedia artist based in Cleveland, OH - but I could be coming to your town, if you'd like.
