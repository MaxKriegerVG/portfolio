Twitch/Youtube
Unity, Photoshop, Illustrator, OBS Studio


aqualounge.tv is my own personal initiative to create an "MTV Liquid Television"-like experience for the streaming age. On Twitch, aqualounge curates a mix of obscure cult classic and brand-new indie game releases alike, mixing in new music and animation during interstitials, as well as some experimental content like real-time Seaman (Dreamcast) with live callers.

I am currently the sole talent behind aqualounge - be it overlay design, technical implementation (Unity and OBS), merchandise designs, promo graphics, the works. I think it represents some of my freshest and most varied visual design work.

Check out the official Linktree for absolutely everything aqualounge - and stay tuned.
