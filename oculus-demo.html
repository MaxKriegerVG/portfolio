Dreamforce (Oculus/Leap Motion)



Dreamforce

(Oculus/Leap Motion Demo)


2016
Oculus Rift DK2 and Leap Motion
Unity


An interactive VR trade show demo I developed for Dreamforce 2016 using an Oculus DK2, a Leap Motion (pre-Orion Drivers), and Unity.

The demo guided users through a hypothetical VR training scenario powered by Salesforce. Users were tasked with assembling a car engine by navigating a gesture-driven interface powered by the Leap Motion, while relying on information provided by real-time Salesforce data (obtained via REST web service) in order to complete the assembly.
