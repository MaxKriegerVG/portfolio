u::r::here

November 2019
Browser and VR
Mozilla Hubs


u::r::here was an experimental virtual museum, event venue, and 3D hangout built using Mozilla Hubs, an A-Frame based virtual space toolset. Alongside freely available models, I myself modeled and designed the museum and venue space using Blender 3D.

Dedicated to the "y2k aesthetic", u::r::here sought to chronologize the internet-based techno-optimism of the late 90's and bring it into the 2020s with 3D galleries and live multiplayer. Its first live concert, u::r::here LIVE, was held in November 2019.

Unfortunately, as of 2024, Hubs has been discontinued by Mozilla, leaving u::r::here lost to the tides of digital ephemera.
